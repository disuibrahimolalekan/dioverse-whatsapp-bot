Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

Dioverse Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") provides WhatsApp automation services for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services, including our WhatsApp bot platform.

Information We Collect

When businesses and their customers interact with our WhatsApp bot service, we may collect:

  • WhatsApp phone numbers used to interact with the bot
  • Message content sent to and received from the bot
  • Business account details provided during onboarding
  • Usage data such as message timestamps and interaction history
  • Payment information processed through our payment partners (Paystack)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and operate the WhatsApp bot service
  • Respond to customer inquiries on behalf of businesses using our platform
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve and troubleshoot our services
  • Send service-related communications to registered businesses

WhatsApp and Meta

Our service operates through the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By interacting with a WhatsApp bot powered by Dioverse, your data is also subject to Meta's Privacy Policy and WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. We only access message data as permitted by Meta's platform policies and as necessary to provide the bot service.

Data Storage and Security

Conversation and customer data is stored securely in cloud databases (MongoDB Atlas). We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Render — cloud hosting for our bot server
  • MongoDB Atlas — database storage
  • Paystack — payment processing
  • Systeme.io — email communications
  • Meta / WhatsApp Business API — message delivery

Each of these services operates under their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered business clients of significant changes via email. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
